Reduce Pain With An Adjustable Bed:

The Infinity Elite Adjustable Bed may provide temporary relief from the symptoms of minor aches and pains due to muscular fatigue or over exertion.

Our Adjustable Beds may temporarily relieve the symptoms of your following health concerns by simply following these methods:


Helping To Relieve Your Symptoms
  • Low Back Pain: Elevate the head of the bed to a 30 degree angle and slightly bend the bed at the knee. This may require minor adjustments until you feel most comfortable.
  • Poor Local Blood Circulation of the Legs: Elevate the lower portion (foot) of the bed from 30 to 45 degrees.
  • Reducing Edema Or Swelling of the Legs: Elevate the lower portion (foot) of the bed from 30 to 45 degrees.
  • Symptoms of Hiatus Hernia: Elevate the head of the bed to a 30
    to 45 degree angle.
  • Symptoms of Gastric Reflux: Elevate the head portion of the bed to a 30 to 45 degree angle.
  • Mild Arthritis and Joint Pain, as well as Muscle Pain Associated with Stress and Tension: Elevate the head of the bed to a 30 degree angle and slightly bend the need at the knee.
  • Night-time Heartburn and Reduce Occasional Snoring: Sleep in an upright position, elevate the head of the bed to a 30 to 45 degree angle.
