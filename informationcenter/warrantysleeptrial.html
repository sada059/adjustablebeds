Frequently Asked (FAQ) Warranty Questions:

Q: What is your warranty on your adjustable beds and adjustable bed mattresses?
A: Our warranty is the best in the business. We are the Factory and offer a FULL lifetime warranty non prorated.

Q: I saw another company online who offers a lifetime warranty, what is the difference? 
A: We both offer a FULL lifetime warranty on all parts including, motors, remotes, mattress, etc... Our prices are lower!

Q: Do you warranty the bed for life? Is it transferable?
A: We warranty the bed and not the person (the buyer) -- so the warranty is transferable. 100% Protection for your investment!

Q: If i have a warranty issue what am i responsible to pay for?
A: All parts including mattress, motors and remote control are covered 100% for life -- and the purchaser pays ONLY for the shipping cost of the part. It's that simple!

Q: Is there anything NOT covered by the warranty? We just want to be safe?
A: This warranty also does not include warranted parts that have been subjected to misuse by the purchaser.

Q: So if we damage the bed ourselves in moving or are over the recommended weight then what?
A: We will still help you with replacing the part needed, but you will have to pay for replacement. We will honor a discount.

Q: Does this warranty cover all products on your website?
A: The Lifetime Warranty covers adjustable beds and adjustable bed mattresses! Airbeds, Memory Foam & Natural Latex come with a 30 year warranty unless purchased with an adjustable bed frame.
 

Frequently Asked (FAQ) Sleep Trial Questions:

Q: Do you have a sleep trial on your adjustable beds & mattresses?
A: Yes, We offer a 120 night sleep trial to make sure your happy. No questions asked.

Q: So i get to try it for 120 nights and what if i don't like the bed?
A: You must try the bed for a minimum 60 days. If you do not like the product you can return it or swap it for a different bed.

Q: Ok so if i don't like the adjustable bed or mattress i can swap for something different?
A: Most people who do not like the product usually have a firmness preference. We try to adjust the feel first before return.

Q: What if i do not like the bed at all and i just want my money back?
A: No problem! Call us and we'll give you an RMA # and once the product is recieved we will credit your credit or debit card.

Q: What fee's am i responsible for? Is there a restocking fee?
A: The only fee's you will be responsible for are the shipping charges which vary from freight company. No restocking fee's.

Q: Why do i have to keep the bed for 60 days? This seems long to us?
A: Sometimes products take a little getting used to because they are so comfy and pressure reducing so we want to be sure you gave it your best effort in getting comfortable with it. Most people are happy within the first night, but some people take longer to adjust.

Q: Is your sleep trial for all products on your site? Some companies only offer it on certain items?
A: The sleep trial is for ALL products except: Pillows, Linens, And any custom made mattresses.

Q: Ok so one more time, If i don't like the bed and return it i do get my money back right?
A: Absolutely correct! You get a credit back in the same manner you paid for the item. Ex: Credit Card
